🌀 Draw in the Air — SEO Plan

From ~500 monthly visitors to 12,000+ in 6 months. Here's exactly how, and why it works.

1 The Problem Today

Draw in the Air is an incredible product — but it's invisible to Google. Here's why:

🔴 No page titles or descriptions

Google doesn't know what each page is about. Every page looks identical.

🔴 JavaScript-only rendering

The app is a React SPA. Google's crawler sees a blank page — it can't read the content.

🔴 No sitemap or robots.txt

Google doesn't know which pages exist. Most pages will never be crawled.

🔴 Slow initial load (~6s LCP)

MediaPipe (8MB AI library) loads immediately, hurting Core Web Vitals and rankings.

💡 Net result: Competitors with no gesture features (ABCMouse, PBS Kids, Starfall) rank for keywords Draw in the Air should own — because nobody has claimed this space yet.

9 What's Ready — You Already Have the Files

All the hard thinking is done. These files are in the zip and ready to drop into the codebase:

✅ SEOMeta.tsx

Drop-in component. Wrap any page with it and it handles titles, descriptions, Open Graph, Twitter, and 7 schema types automatically.

✅ LetterTracePage.tsx

One file generates all 26 letter pages. Add a route per letter and Google sees 26 unique, optimised pages.

✅ useMediaPipeLazy.ts

Defers 8MB AI library until needed. Likely moves LCP from 6s to under 2s — a direct ranking signal improvement.

✅ generate-sitemap.ts

Run once at build time to generate sitemap.xml with all 50+ URLs. Submit to Google Search Console and you're indexed within days.

✅ seo-config.ts

Central store for all page copy, keywords, and meta. Change one file to update all 50 pages — no hunting through components.

✅ vercel.json

Production-ready headers, caching rules, and redirects. Prevents duplicate content and tells browsers to cache assets aggressively.

Bottom line: Draw in the Air sits in a blue ocean. No competitor owns "air drawing for kids." These files, deployed in the right order over 90 days, can turn that advantage into 12,000 monthly free visitors — parents and teachers who are already looking for exactly this.
