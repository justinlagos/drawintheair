## Detailed Description
*There is a 16,000 character limit, but keep it concise and readable. Use markdown/formatting where the dashboard allows.*

**Draw in the Air** is an interactive, browser-based learning platform that lets children draw, trace, and play games using only their hands. 

Built for modern browsers and Chromebooks, the app uses advanced, local-only webcam tracking to translate hand gestures into on-screen actions. Kids can pinch to draw, trace the alphabet, and pop bubbles without touching a mouse, trackpad, or screen.

**Key Features:**
• **Free Paint Mode:** Draw anything with your finger in the air using vibrant colors and particle effects.
• **Educational Tracing:** Learn to write by tracing letters (A-Z), numbers, and shapes with real-time visual feedback.
• **Interactive Games:** Play "Bubble Pop" and "Sort & Place" to develop hand-eye coordination and spatial reasoning.
• **Pinch-to-Draw Control:** Intuitive gesture system—pinch your thumb and index finger to draw, open your hand to pause.
• **School & Chromebook Ready:** Runs smoothly in the browser, making it perfect for classroom deployment and at-home learning.

**Privacy by Design:**
We take student privacy seriously. All camera processing happens entirely locally on your device using on-device AI — we do not record, save, store, or transmit any video footage. No personal data is collected. The platform requires no accounts, and children cannot be identified. We use anonymised, first-party analytics only (no Google Analytics, no ad networks).

**How this extension works:**
This is a lightweight launcher extension. Clicking the icon will instantly open the complete Draw in the Air web application in a new tab. The extension requires zero browser permissions and collects no data.
