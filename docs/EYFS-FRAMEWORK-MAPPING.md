# EYFS Framework Mapping

This document provides the direct mapping of game levels to the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) 2024 statutory framework.

## Phonics Sky Writer

| Level | Game Action | EYFS Statement Mapping |
|-------|-------------|------------------------|
| L1 | Sky Strokes (Lines/Curves) | **PD**: "Use large-muscle movements to wave flags and streamers, paint and make marks." |
| L2 | Letter Trails (A–Z) | **L**: "Write some or all of their name. Write some letters accurately." |
| L3 | Sound & Write (Phonemes) | **L**: "Say a sound for each letter in the alphabet and at least 10 digraphs." |

## Sorting Garden

| Level | Game Action | EYFS Statement Mapping |
|-------|-------------|------------------------|
| L1 | Living vs. Not Living | **UW**: "Explore the natural world around them, making observations and drawing pictures of animals and plants." |
| L2 | Nature Categories | **UW**: "Understand the effect of changing seasons on the natural world around them." |
| L3 | Garden Challenges (Logic) | **M**: "Continue, copy and create repeating patterns. Compare length, weight and capacity." |

## Maze Game

| Level | Game Action | EYFS Statement Mapping |
|-------|-------------|------------------------|
| L1 | Straight Paths | **PD**: "Develop their small motor skills so that they can use a range of tools competently." |
| L2 | Turns & Curves | **PSED**: "Be confident to try new activities and show independence, resilience and perseverance." |
| L3 | Thinking Mazes | **M**: "Understand position through words alone – for example, 'The bag is under the table' – with no pointing." |

## Key

- **PD**: Physical Development
- **L**: Literacy
- **UW**: Understanding the World
- **M**: Mathematics
- **PSED**: Personal, Social and Emotional Development

---

*This mapping ensures all game modes align with the EYFS 2024 statutory framework requirements for early years education.*
